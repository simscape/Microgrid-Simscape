Components

The custom components in this section enable you to model different types of microgrids and analyze different renewable integration challeges. You will find models that help you build large wind/solar farms along with their associated controls.
The custom library for microgrid and renewables has these categorization :
  1. Sources
  2. Storage
  3. Control

The Wind Power Plant component represents wind turbines with aerodynamic and electrical dynamics. It includes generator control and power electronic interfaces.

The Generator component models synchronous generators for conventional generation and hybrid systems. It supports dynamic simulations for voltage, frequency, and fault response.
